News

Stay updated with the latest at Mutonda’s Bakery, where we're always cooking up something new!

Seasonal Menu Launch

As the seasons change, so does our menu! We’re thrilled to announce our latest seasonal offerings, featuring ingredients that celebrate the flavors of the season. From pumpkin spice scones in the fall to fresh strawberry tarts in the spring, our seasonal menu brings the best of local produce straight to your table. Visit us in-store or check out our online shop to explore the full range of our new creations.

Baking Classes Return

Due to popular demand, our in-person baking classes are back! Whether you’re a seasoned baker looking to refine your skills or a complete beginner wanting to learn the basics, our classes have something for everyone. Our expert bakers will guide you through hands-on lessons in bread making, pastry creation, and cake decorating. Spaces are limited, so be sure to sign up early! For more information and to register, visit our website or drop by the bakery.

One Year of Community Impact

This month, we’re celebrating a milestone—one year since the launch of our vocational training program! Over the past year, we’ve had the privilege of working with individuals from diverse backgrounds, helping them gain valuable skills and confidence in the workforce. To mark this special occasion, we’re hosting an open house at the bakery. Come meet the participants, hear their stories, and see firsthand the impact our community initiatives are having. We couldn’t have done it without your support—thank you for being part of the Mutonda family!

Holiday Specials

The holidays are just around the corner, and we’ve got something special planned for each one! From Thanksgiving pies to Christmas cookie boxes, our holiday specials are a beloved tradition at Mutonda’sbakery. Pre-orders will be opening soon, so keep an eye out for announcements on our website and social media. Our holiday offerings are made in limited batches, so be sure to reserve yours early!
